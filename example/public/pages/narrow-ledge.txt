You decide to edge across the narrow ledge.

It's not so narrow after all, and you are crossing it with ease, when a bright glimmer catches the corner of your eye.

"Is that... gold?" you think, and turn to face it, forgetting that you are on a ridge of stone no more than a few feet wide.

You slip and tumble, pointlessly grasping at the slick, mocking limestone surface. You tumble down endlessly.
